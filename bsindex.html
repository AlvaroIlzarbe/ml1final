Official Pokémon Games Outside the Main Series

This type of game includes a collection of titles that offer different features compared to the main series. In these games, players explore a new region while facing off against a villainous team. However, unlike the main series, the player's goal is not to defeat a series of gym leaders and the Elite Four. Instead, the primary mission is to stop the villainous team and save the world. These games stand out due to their unique themes, ranging from capturing opponents' Pokémon to using a special device that creates circles to capture them.

Pokémon Colosseum

Release Year 2003
Generation Third
Console Nintendo GameCube
Features First 3D Pokémon game with double battles as the standard. Introduces Shadow Pokémon and their purification.
Summary In the Orre region, a former member of Team Snagem teams up with a young girl to purify Shadow Pokémon captured by the villainous Cipher organization.

Pokémon XD: Gale of Darkness

Release Year 2005
Generation Third
Console Nintendo GameCube
Features Sequel to Colosseum, with improved purification and a deeper narrative.
Summary Five years after Colosseum, Cipher returns with Shadow Lugia, and a young hero must stop their plans and purify corrupted Pokémon.

Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Blue Rescue Team & Red Rescue Team

Release Year 2005
Generation Third
Console Nintendo DS (Blue), Game Boy Advance (Red)
Features Roguelike system where you control Pokémon in dungeons.
Summary A human transformed into a Pokémon leads a rescue team, exploring dungeons and uncovering how to reverse the transformation.

Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Explorers of Time, Darkness & Sky

Release Year 2007–2009
Generation Fourth
Console Nintendo DS
Features Expanded narrative and mechanics with a more complex story.
Summary Alongside your partner, you explore dungeons to protect the world from temporal collapse and confront a treacherous future.

Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Gates to Infinity

Release Year 2012
Generation Fifth
Console Nintendo 3DS
Features First 3D game with a focus on community building.
Summary You build a Pokémon paradise while facing a threat that endangers the peace of the Pokémon world.

Pokémon Mystery Dungeon: Super Mystery Dungeon

Release Year 2015
Generation Sixth
Console Nintendo 3DS
Features Expanded content to include all Pokémon up to the Sixth Generation.
Summary You investigate mysterious petrifications while forming alliances with legendary Pokémon to save the world.
