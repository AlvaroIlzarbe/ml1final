What Are Fangames?

Fangames are fan-created Pokémon games, made by fans for fans, without any profit motive. These games are often created using RPG Maker, allowing fans to introduce, create, or modify Pokémon images and styles, either from the existing generations or custom-made. Fangames bring creative experiences, including new regions, storylines, gameplay mechanics, and original or existing Pokémon.

Different Types of Fangames

Type Characteristics Examples Image
RPG Traditional Inspired by the core gameplay with exploration, gyms, trainers, and Pokémon capture. Pokémon Uranium, Pokémon Añil
Roguelike Randomly generated dungeons, fighting Pokémon, item finding, and resource management. Pokémon Reminiscence
HackRooms Modifying ROMs of official games, adding new regions, difficulty, and challenges. Pokémon Light Platinum, Pokémon Radical RED
MMORPG Massively multiplayer games allowing players to connect globally for events. Pokémon MMO 3D
Survival Surviving in a dangerous Pokémon world, exploring, managing resources, and intense combat. Pokémon Reborn
Fusion or Cross-genres Combines Pokémon with other genres like platforms, real-time strategy, or deck-building. Pokémon Infinite Fusion, Pokémon Conquest-like
